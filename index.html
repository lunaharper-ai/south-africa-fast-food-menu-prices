Overview of South Africa’s Fast Food Market

South Africa has one of the most diverse fast food industries in Africa. From globally recognized brands to strong local chains, pricing structures vary depending on location, ingredient sourcing, and operational costs.

This guide provides an overview of menu price ranges, popular categories, and how major chains structure their meals. Prices are generally listed in South African Rand (R) and may vary by city or branch.
